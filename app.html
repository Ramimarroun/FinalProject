Registration

When you first open the app you will see the login screen with the option to sign-up. Here you will be asked to enter the appropriate fields shown below. We also support sign up with Facebook accounts. After clicking the sign up button with the Facebook option, a pop-up will appear asking you to agree that App wants to log in with your Facebook account.The app will only display your first name.

Registration as advisor

If you are a professional and would like to be a volunteer, you can send us a registration form. After you have completed your registration as an advisor, we will let you know as soon as possible once we have reviewed it.
By choosing the country, will the app automatically ask you if you would like to change the language to your country's language.

NB!

Volunteer must have a specific personal or professional interest related to one of the legal or psychological counseling, so you need to upload your documents that prove your competence. All documents must be less than 3MB in size and must be in one of the following formats: GIF, JPG, JPEG, PNG, PDF, TXT or Word (.doc or .docx).

Login

Once you sing in, it will open the home page where you can see many shared experiences of VAW. You can click plus button to share your experience or others experiences of VAW. In addition you can click on anonymous mode button to hide your real identity if you would like.

Menu

By clicking on the menu, you will get more alternatives. You can choose whatever you want.

New Case

You can describe your case and we will refer it to our specialists. Then she or he will contact you as soon as possible.

psychological counselling

You can choose either to chat or call a psychological specialist. All our psychological specialists work voluntarily.

Legal advice

Once you click on legal advice, You can choose also either to chat or call one of our .All our legal specialists work voluntarily.

Calling

The call will be secret and secure from the application directly, so you do not need to add your telephone number.

help

By help can user review the most frequent questions, and there will be a choice to " get in touch" whereby can the user see the address, e-mail and telephone of the organization "WAV".

help

On "get in touch" page user can send a direct message to the center to wondering about some question which is not listed infrequent questions or for report a necessary case, or to submit a complaint regarding the consultations, or regarding the response or comments of other users.
