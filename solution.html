combating violence against women

Our idea sets out to contribute in combating violence against women. By creating a website and mobile application, we publish womensâ€™ personal experiences of violence, which the user chooses to publish anonymously or not with a specific hashtag to support the case, and to provide advice to other people on how to act in such cases. The user can receive responses from other users as well.

Through the system psychologists, legal and health professionals can provide guidance and support through their own control panel through which they can answer users' inquiries. Registration of specialists in the system is not done randomly, as those interested (volunteers) must provide the site management with certificates or documents that prove their competence.

Our app combating VAW contributes to estimating the cases and causes of violence against women around the world. Many cases of violence against women go unreported. Shame, fear and lack of knowledge about rights make women reluctant to report incidents to the police. The app can help us to gather information about VAW in these countries where women are exposed to violence without reporting.

100% FREE & NO ADS:

Combating VAW is free and will always be free. We are not going to sell ads or introduce subscription fees.
